# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Compile designs biological brains. The weight of that work demands a community built on respect, honesty, and responsibility.

## Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive
- Report negative results as readily as positive ones
- Credit prior work accurately
- Accept constructive feedback
- Prioritize safety and ethical considerations in all contributions

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Misrepresenting results or data
- Submitting work that intentionally circumvents safety considerations

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces: GitHub, discussions, pull requests, and any community channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to founders@compile.now. All complaints will be reviewed. The project team will take appropriate action, up to and including permanent removal from the community.
